About Aubrey H. Laverty

Aubrey H. Laverty is currently a senior consultant at Bravo Consulting Group, LLC. At Bravo, she works with clients to develop solutions that improve business processes and increase collaboration. Aubrey has built a career creating IT solutions that are easy-to-use for all.

Aubrey is a graduate of the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, Michigan. She received her Bachelors of Arts in Sociology - Social Inequality: Race, Class, and Gender, with a minor in Mathematics.
